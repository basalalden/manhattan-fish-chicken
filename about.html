Our Story

From One Kitchen to Four Locations

Manhattan Fish & Chicken started in 2009 with a simple mission: serve the freshest, most delicious fried chicken and fish to our community. What began as a small family restaurant in Harlem has grown into four thriving locations across New York City.

Our founder, inspired by generations of family recipes, wanted to bring authentic, home-style cooking to the neighborhood. Every piece of chicken is hand-breaded, every fish fillet is seasoned with our secret blend of spices, and every customer is treated like family.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers daily while maintaining the same commitment to quality and freshness that we started with over 15 years ago.

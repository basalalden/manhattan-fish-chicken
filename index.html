Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 2009

For over 15 years, Manhattan Fish & Chicken has been serving the community with the freshest, most delicious fried chicken and seafood. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a beloved local destination with multiple locations.

We're not just a restaurant - we're also a full-service fish and poultry market where you can buy fresh seafood and meats to cook at home. Quality and freshness are at the heart of everything we do.
